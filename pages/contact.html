Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday through Sunday, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. For urgent inquiries outside these hours, please send us an email and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Do you offer delivery for bulk orders?

Yes, we provide delivery service for bulk orders throughout Metro Manila and nearby provinces. Contact our sales team for delivery logistics and cost details.

Are your products quality guaranteed?

All our products undergo rigorous quality control and testing to ensure the highest standards. Quality is guaranteed through our certification and testing protocols.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash & bank transfer. For bulk orders and corporate accounts, please contact us to discuss custom payment arrangements.

Our team can recommend the right fertilizer based on your crop type and soil conditions. Contact us with details about what you're growing and we'll provide expert guidance.

Yes! Our agricultural experts are available to provide guidance on proper fertilizer application, crop management, and soil health. Contact us for consultation.
