ABOUT US

Ang Agri-Victorious Trading Corporation ay isa sa pinagkakatiwalaang supplier ng premium quality fertilizers at agricultural products sa buong Pilipinas. Nakatuon kami sa pagbibigay ng pinakamahusay na serbisyo at produkto sa aming mga valued customers at farming communities.

Agri-Victorious Trading Corporation is one of the most trusted suppliers of premium-quality fertilizers and agricultural products throughout the Philippines. We are committed to providing the best service and products to our valued customers and farming communities.

Sa loob ng higit pitong taon, kami ay naging mahalagang bahagi ng paglaki ng agricultural sector sa Pilipinas, na tumutulong sa mga magsasaka at agricultural professionals na makamit ang mas mataas na ani at mas magandang kalidad ng produkto.

For more than seven years, we have been an integral part of the agricultural sector's growth in the Philippines, helping farmers and agricultural professionals achieve higher yields and superior product quality.

OUR MISSION

Magbigay ng de-kalidad na pataba at agricultural products na sumusuporta sa sustainable at productive farming practices sa buong bansa, na nagbibigay kapangyarihan sa mga magsasaka na makamit ang mas mataas na ani at mas magandang kalidad ng produkto.

To provide quality fertilizers and agricultural products that support sustainable and productive farming practices throughout the country, empowering farmers to achieve higher yields and better product quality. We believe in balancing agricultural productivity with environmental stewardship for a sustainable future.

OUR VISION

Maging nangungunang supplier ng agricultural products na kinikilala sa buong bansa at pandaigdig dahil sa aming dedikasyon sa kalidad, innovation, at exceptional customer service sa industriya ng pataba at agrikultura.

To be the leading agricultural product supplier recognized nationwide and globally for our dedication to quality, innovation, and exceptional customer service in the fertilizer and agricultural industry. We envision a thriving agricultural sector where every farmer has access to premium products and expert support.

🌾 Aming Dedikasyon sa Agrikultura

Ang pataba ay may mahalaga papel sa kalusugan ng lupa at produktibidad ng ani. Ang tatlong pangunahing nutrients sa mga pataba—nitrogen, phosphorus, at potassium—ay essential para sa paglaki ng halaman at development. Bawat isa sa aming mga produkto ay formulated na may tamang balance ng mga nutrients na ito upang maximize ang paglaki ng ani at production. Nauunawaan namin ang mga hamon na kinakaharap ng mga magsasaka at nagbibigay ng tailored solutions para sa iba't ibang ani at growing conditions.

Fertilizers play a crucial role in soil health and crop productivity. The three primary nutrients in fertilizers—nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium—are essential for plant growth and development. Each of our products is formulated with the correct balance of these nutrients to maximize crop growth and production. We understand the challenges farmers face and provide tailored solutions for different crops and growing conditions.
