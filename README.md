# Agri-Victorious Trading Corporation

## 🌾 Premium Fertilizer Solutions Website

A modern, responsive, and bilingual (Tagalog/English) website for Agri-Victorious Trading Corporation, showcasing premium fertilizer products and agricultural solutions.

### 🎯 Features

- **Responsive Design** - Mobile-first approach works perfectly on all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- **Bilingual Support** - Full Tagalog and English content throughout the site
- **Product Showcase** - Professional product catalog with images and specifications
- **Modern UI** - Beautiful gradients, animations, and intuitive navigation
- **Professional Brand** - Color scheme based on company logo with gold accents
- **Quotation System** - Contact-based quotation system for product inquiries
- **Accessibility** - Semantic HTML and proper contrast for readability

### 📱 Pages

- **Home** - Hero section with featured products and "Why Choose Us" benefits
- **Products** - Complete fertilizer product catalog (NPK 14-14-14, NPK 16-20-0, NPK 21-0-0+24S, Urea 46-0-0)
- **About Us** - Company information, mission, vision, and core values
- **Resources** - Agricultural resources and farming guides
- **Contact** - Quotation request and inquiry form

### 🎨 Design Highlights

- **Color Palette** - Green (#1b6b2f) and Gold (#d4af37) from company branding
- **Typography** - Clean, readable fonts with proper sizing hierarchy
- **Animations** - Smooth transitions and hover effects throughout
- **Mobile Optimization** - Breakpoints at 768px, 480px, and 320px
